# Trade Terms Agreement — Meridian Foods & Harvest & Co
Effective 2025-03-01. Supersedes all prior trade agreements.

## 1. Payment Terms
### 1.1 Terms
Net 45 from invoice date.

## 2. Scan Allowances
### 2.1 Scan-Based Allowances
Scan allowances are payable per unit scanned at point of sale during the promotional period, at the authorized rate, up to the promotion's funding cap.
### 2.2 Scan Data
Harvest & Co provides weekly scan data for promoted items during and for two weeks following each promotional period.

## 3. Promotional Performance & Documentation
### 3.1 Authorization
Promotional deductions must reference a Meridian promotion authorization.
### 3.2 Billback Claims
Billback claims must state the events or units billed and the applicable authorized rate.
### 3.3 Claim Timing
Promotional claims must be submitted within 90 days of the promotion end date.
### 3.4 Demo and Display Proof of Performance
Demo and display billbacks require a signed Proof-of-Performance form (Exhibit B) for each event and a store execution report, submitted within 30 days of the event. Claims lacking Exhibit B documentation are not payable as submitted and are subject to Meridian review.

## 4. Audit
### 4.1 Look-Back Period
Either party may audit promotional payments and deductions for 18 months following the close of the applicable fiscal quarter.

## 5. Disputes
### 5.1 Escalation Path
Unresolved disputes escalate to the Harvest & Co grocery buyer and Meridian regional sales manager.
