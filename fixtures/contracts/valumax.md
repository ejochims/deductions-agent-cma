# Trade Terms Agreement — Meridian Foods & ValuMax
Effective 2025-01-15. Supersedes all prior trade agreements.

## 1. Payment Terms
### 1.1 Terms
2% 10, Net 30. ValuMax settles invoices on consolidated weekly remittances.

## 2. Promotional Allowances
### 2.1 Scan-Based Allowances
Scan allowances are payable per unit scanned at point of sale, at the authorized rate, up to the promotion's funding cap.
### 2.2 Billbacks
Feature and ad billbacks are payable at the authorized rate per the promotion authorization. Claims must state the units or events billed and the applicable rate.
### 2.3 Promotional Period
Scan allowances apply only to units scanned within the promotional period stated on the promotion authorization. Units scanned outside that period are not eligible for reimbursement.

## 3. Deductions
### 3.1 Authorization Required
ValuMax may deduct only against promotions pre-approved in writing by Meridian and bearing a Meridian promotion ID. Deductions lacking a valid authorization are repayable upon notice.
### 3.2 Documentation
Deduction backup must identify the promotion, items, period, and calculation.
### 3.3 Offsets
Meridian may offset invalid deductions against future ValuMax promotional payments after the dispute process concludes.

## 4. New Item Allowances
### 4.1 Slotting Amount
New item slotting is $12,000 per item for chainwide placement, invoiced upon item setup.
### 4.2 One-Time Charge
Slotting is a one-time charge per item. No additional slotting may be deducted for the same item absent a new written authorization (e.g., for a packaging change requiring new item setup).

## 5. Marketing Development Fund (MDF)
### 5.1 Accrual
ValuMax accrues MDF at 2% of net purchases of Meridian products, available for qualifying marketing activities.
### 5.2 Qualifying Activities
Qualifying MDF activities are: weekly print circular features, in-store display placements, end-cap programs, and in-store demo events. MDF claims must itemize the activity, dates, and items supported.
### 5.3 Claims Procedure
MDF claims are submitted quarterly with supporting documentation and are subject to Meridian review before deduction.

## 6. Audit
### 6.1 Look-Back Period
Either party may audit promotional payments and deductions for 36 months following the applicable fiscal year end.
