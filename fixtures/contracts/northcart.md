# Trade Terms Agreement — Meridian Foods & NorthCart
Effective 2025-02-01. Supersedes all prior trade agreements.

## 1. Payment Terms
### 1.1 Terms
Net 30 from invoice date. Early-payment discount of 1% for payment within 10 days.
### 1.2 Deduction Settlement
Deductions taken by NorthCart against Meridian invoices are settled through Meridian's deduction review process within 60 days of remittance.

## 2. Promotional Allowances
### 2.1 Scan-Based Allowances
Scan allowances are payable per unit scanned at point of sale during the agreed promotional period, at the rate stated on the promotion authorization, up to the promotion's funding cap.
### 2.2 Display and Feature Billbacks
Display and feature billbacks are payable per the promotion authorization. Proof of performance (signed display agreement and circular tear sheet or equivalent) must accompany the claim.

## 3. Deduction Rights & Documentation
### 3.1 Authorization
NorthCart may deduct only against promotions authorized in writing by Meridian sales management, referencing the Meridian promotion ID.
### 3.2 Backup Documentation
Each deduction must include backup documentation sufficient to identify the promotion, period, items, and calculation of the amount taken.

## 4. New Item Slotting
### 4.1 Slotting Schedule
For each authorized new item: regional distribution phase (up to 900 stores) — $9,500 per item; chainwide expansion phase — $42,000 per item, invoiced upon distribution confirmation.
### 4.2 Invoicing
Slotting is deducted in the period the corresponding distribution phase is confirmed. Each phase may be deducted once per item.

## 5. Audit Window
### 5.1 Look-Back Period
Either party may audit promotional payments and deductions for 24 months following the close of the applicable fiscal quarter.

## 6. Dispute Resolution
### 6.1 Escalation Path
Unresolved deduction disputes escalate to the NorthCart category manager and Meridian sales director for joint review within 30 days.
